Feature Item Princess Hoodie

Description

Blimp original medium weight jacquard knit hoodie for french bulldogs. Let these cute playful monsters join your frenchies daily adventures! Three-dimensional tailoring around shoulder and armpit to maintain and protect natural movement. Serged seam ribbed trim finish around neck, sleeve and waist for durability. Perfect for the cold winter season.

Details

  • Designed and handcrafted by MandyMinh
  • 80% acrylic, 15% nylon, 5% wool
  • Faux fur lining
  • Also available in pink
